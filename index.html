I'm an artificial intelligence enthusiast. After taking many Machine Learning courses, I decided to make a project that would combine my knowledge in the area with things I appreciate and have interest on, and at the same time, be of use to people, thus this AI project was made.

This is the first full stack, AI-driven, container-based, cloud-based project I did. Actually, it's the first full project I've ever done alone, so feel free to share your thoughts, critics, advices, bugs and funny results on my e-mail, LinkedIn or Reddit post.

I used two Natural Language Processing (NLP) models. The first one is to search for semantic similarity between the user's "keywords" and the 350+ videos' transcripts, and returns the top10 results. The second one is a QuestionAnswering model that extracts answers from top1 result according to the user's "question"
